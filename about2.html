about

Atria Foundation is the philanthropic arm of the Atria Group, a South Indian conglomerate with 70+ years of expertise in hospitality, education, power generation and infrastructure.

Humane for Humanity

The Atria Foundation was founded during the COVID-19 pandemic, a time when society at large was called upon to extend beyond self-centeredness and lend a helping hand to those severely impacted by the crisis.
The Foundation emerged as a beacon of support, embodying the collective efforts needed to navigate the challenges and disruptions faced by thousands in the community.

Milestone : Atria Foundation has served 1,00,00,000 meals till date & is continuing...

Our Objective: No One goes Hungry

Mission

At the heart of Atria Foundation's mission is a commitment to leveraging technology & the power of collective action to eliminate disparities in access to basic necessities. Through our collaborative initiatives for common good, we strive to create a world where essential resources are universally accessible and communities are sustainable.

Vision

A future where the essentials of life—food, health, and education—are not just privileges but rights for every individual.

Our initiatives

Our initiatives align with the Sustainable development goals (SDGs) that secure the rights and well-being of everyone on a healthy, thriving planet.
